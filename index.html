About Me

What an adventure. . . starting a coding career after some forty plus year in Mechanical Engineering. Who knows were this will take me. I started "On the Board" and moved into computerized drawing in the mid 80's. The software has become so sophisticated, I stop from time to time and am amazed of what I can create in such a short time. But it's time to move to the software side of things. Being around programmers I appreciate the ease of change and the instant gratification when changes workout.

And here I am, a Full Stack Developer in training. I truly enjoy the mechanics of the whole system. Multiple parts all doing there piece to make the whole thing work. Kind of mechanical. So I guess I won't be to far away from my roots!
